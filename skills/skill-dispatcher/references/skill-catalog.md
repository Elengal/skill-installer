## Selection hints

### Multiple skills match — priority order

1. **Vendor-official beats community.** `openai-yeet` > `finish-branch`; `vercel-react-best-practices` > `toolkit-react-patterns`; `supabase-supabase-postgres-best-practices` > `toolkit-postgres-optimization`.
2. **Specificity wins.** `toolkit-postgres-optimization` > `db-optimize` > `toolkit-database-optimization`.
3. **Anthropic-official > community** for general principles. `anthropic-frontend-design` > `kc-frontend-design`.
4. **Stack match wins.** `toolkit-nextjs-mastery` > `fullstack-dev` > `kc-frontend-design`.
5. **Scope match wins.** `artes-executing-plans` (full workflow) > `tdd` (point tool).
6. **Recency wins** when unsure.
